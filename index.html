Welcome to the orientation scavenger hunt!

This scavenger hunt will familiarize you with the locations of the university of the virgin islands.

Rules

  1. Split into even groups
  2. Your group has 2 minutes to attempt to solve the riddles, the answer will lead you to a location in the school. Take your time to ensure you get the most appropriate answer
  3. At each location take a picture with your group.
  4. Remeber to be calm and orderly!
